About EduLock

Modernizing online learning with focus and discipline

Our Mission

At EduLock, our mission is simple: to help students and professionals stay focused, learn effectively, and build lifelong learning habits. We believe that with the right tools, anyone can overcome distractions, stay disciplined, and achieve their academic and personal goals.

Our Vision

To become the ultimate study companion for millions of students and professionals worldwide, transforming the way people learn online by combining innovation, usability, and impact.

What We Offer

  • •

    AI-driven focus management: Real-time nudges to keep users on track and minimize distractions.

  • •

    Time-locked study sessions: Goal-oriented sessions that enforce discipline and commitment.

  • •

    AI-generated exit tests: Quick quizzes to validate learning and ensure comprehension.

Our Story

EduLock started as a college project during a hackathon. As students ourselves, we struggled with staying focused during online classes and study sessions. We noticed how distractions like social media and games ruined our productivity, and how traditional platforms like Zoom or Google Classroom didn't help us stay on track.

That's when we thought: what if there was a tool that not only blocks distractions but also helps us learn better? That's how EduLock was born—a platform that combines AI-powered focus management, personalized insights, and real-time monitoring to make learning more effective and enjoyable.
